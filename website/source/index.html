About the company
    Shasta Exchange is a federally registered business of the United States of America as a sole proprietorship. The mission of the company is to enable the psychological effects that paper based media provides; However the products are provided in digital format although may not always be in digital format.

This is intended to be achieved by discovering new algorithms that enforce inferred paper based digital effects.

The company is currently focused on enabling a large amount of individuals to be able to have access at becoming a digital publisher; With the secondary focus being of paper based digital solutions pertaining to financial planning.
